Ethereum on SOL

This is the official whitepaper for the project Ethereum on SOL. A project that aims to relive the early days of Ethereum PoS while teaching the community about Proof-of-Stake.
In 2015, Ethereum introduced a revolutionary concept—a decentralized platform for creating and executing smart contracts and decentralized applications (DApps). Ethereum's vision of a world computer, fueled by its native cryptocurrency Ether (ETH), has inspired a wave of innovation in the blockchain space. Our project, deeply rooted in Ethereum's principles, brings forward significant innovations that embrace the progress in blockchain technology and address the evolving needs of the market.
A pivotal innovation in our project is the integration of Solana's blockchain technology, specifically harnessing Non-Fungible Tokens (NFTs) as a fundamental component of the proof-of-stake (PoS) mining process. Solana's blockchain, known for high throughput and low transaction fees, provides an efficient platform for handling a large number of transactions. By incorporating Solana NFTs, we leverage the efficiency of the Solana network while introducing a novel dimension to the staking process.
In this revised Ethereum system, staking is no longer contingent on extensive staking of ETH. Instead, it is linked to unique digital assets—Solana NFTs—each contributing a nodes validator. This approach democratizes the staking process, allowing individuals to participate by owning these NFTs. The system is capped at 10,000 stakers, represented by the limited number of NFTs, ensuring decentralized control over the Ethereum network.
Moreover, the integration of NFTs into the staking process introduces a new dynamic in the distribution of staking rewards. Each staking validator contribute to the network security, so each one receives an equal share of the rewards.
In conclusion, our project aligns with the original vision of Ethereum POS, adapted for the modern era. By integrating Solana NFTs into the staking process and adjusting key parameters such as block time and reward distribution, we aim to create a more accessible, efficient, and equitable blockchain network. This network upholds the principles of decentralization and trustless transactions while reflecting the technological advancements and evolving needs of today's digital world within the Ethereum ecosystem.
